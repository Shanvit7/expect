# Interview: Dennis Brotzky

**Role:** Co-founder of Fey, works mainly as an engineer.

Dennis Brotzky is an engineer who loves design. He is the co-founder of Fey, an app that helps you make better investments.

## Key Topics

### About Fey

- Fey is built by a small team: Dennis (engineering), Thiago Costa (design), and Thomas Russell (engineering/product/admin).
- Small team, big ambitions - focused on creating a high-quality investment tool.

### Design engineering philosophy

- Comes from the design engineering "ish" category on Twitter.
- Believes in the intersection of engineering and design - engineers who care deeply about design quality.
- The craft of building great interfaces requires both technical skill and design sensibility.

### On building products

- A small, focused team can build remarkable things when everyone cares about quality.
- Engineering decisions should serve the user experience, with design quality being a first-class concern.
