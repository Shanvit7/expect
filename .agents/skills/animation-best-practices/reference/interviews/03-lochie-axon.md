# Interview: Lochie Axon

**Role:** Design engineer at Avara. Built the Family site, docs, ConnectKit, and Avara's site.

Lochie is a design engineer who built the Family site and many other notable projects. In this conversation they discuss how Lochie got into design engineering, how he thinks about animations, and how he builds them.

## Key Topics

### Early animation roots

- Started with animation as a kid with flip books and stop motion using his dad's camcorder.
- First experience with coding animation was a turtle program where you animate on screen with code (Logo).
- Has always been drawn to animation in all its forms.

### Design engineering approach

- Builds with deep attention to craft and detail.
- The Family site is a notable example of high-quality design engineering with thoughtful animations.

### How he thinks about animations

- Animations should serve the user experience, not just look cool.
- Attention to subtle details and polish makes the difference between good and great work.
- ConnectKit is an example of creating delightful, well-animated developer tools.
