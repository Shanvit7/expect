# Timing

## rules

- MUST post product launches at 8-9:30 AM PT on weekdays (Tue-Fri)
- SHOULD post opinions/hot takes at 2-6 PM PT (8,600 avg likes vs 4,800 for morning)
- SHOULD post personal stories Saturday evening ~7 PM PT
- NEVER launch on Sunday (2,500 avg likes vs 6,800 on Tuesday)
- SHOULD schedule posts (timestamps show :01, :08 — not manual)

## data

| content type     | time (PT) | day               |
| ---------------- | --------- | ----------------- |
| product launch   | 9 AM      | Tue-Fri           |
| opinion/hot take | 2-6 PM    | any weekday       |
| personal story   | 7 PM      | Saturday          |
| meme             | any       | any except Sunday |

9 AM PT catches US East Coast lunch (12 PM ET) + US West Coast morning + European evening.

## weekly cadence

```
Mon-Fri morning:     product features, dev tips
Weekday afternoon:   1-2 industry opinions per week
Weekend:             1 meme per week
Monthly:             1 personal story (Saturday evening)
```
