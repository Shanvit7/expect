# Sustained Campaign

The launch tweet is not the strategy. The 2-3 week campaign is.

- react-scan launch tweet: 572.8K views
- 3-week campaign (14 tweets): 4.9M total views, 300+ stars/day for 18 days
- react-grab with no campaign: peak 428 stars/day, baseline (<20) within a week

## rules

- MUST plan 10-14 follow-up posts, not just a launch tweet
- MUST use the "scanning Company X" template — single most replicable high-engagement format
- SHOULD ship a new feature daily during days 2-5 and tweet about it
- SHOULD mix 60% company scans, 20% feature demos, 20% memes during campaign
- SHOULD space tweets 4-6 hours apart (algo applies exponential decay for repeated same-author appearances)

## "scanning Company X" template

1. Use your tool on a famous product (GitHub, Slack, TikTok, Pinterest)
2. Find something embarrassing/surprising
3. Gentle tone, not hostile ("hey pinterest, we need to talk")
4. Video showing the tool on their site
5. Tag the company

**Results:** TikTok 1.1M, Pinterest 815K, X/Twitter 779K, GitHub 767K, Slack 285K views.

Works because each post is simultaneously a product demo AND social content. People quote-tweet "do my company next" — organic engagement loop.

## structure

```
Day 1:      Launch tweet
Days 2-5:   Ship + tweet a new feature daily
Days 5-20:  "Scanning Company X" posts, mixed with memes and integration demos
```
