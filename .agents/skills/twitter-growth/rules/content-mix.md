# Content Mix

## the flywheel

```
Engine 1: Industry opinions  → builds followers (top-of-funnel)
Engine 2: Product launches   → converts followers into users/stars
Engine 3: External coverage  → step-function growth (can't control this)
```

Skip Engine 1 → audience caps at current followers.
Skip Engine 2 → audience entertained but never converts.

## rules

- MUST post 2-3 industry opinions/week between launches — most efficient follower growth vehicle
- MUST post product launches from personal account, not company account (316x difference)
- SHOULD post 1 meme/week — top-of-funnel, grows followers, but B/L is 0.08-0.15
- SHOULD post articles as native tweet (key insight + screenshot) with link in reply — direct article links get capped distribution
- SHOULD post 1 personal story/month max — loses impact with frequency

## engagement by type

| type                | avg likes | avg B/L   | purpose                     |
| ------------------- | --------- | --------- | --------------------------- |
| mega-viral opinions | 5K-25K    | low       | follower growth             |
| product launches    | 3K-10K    | 0.53-1.07 | user/star conversion        |
| memes               | 1K-4K     | 0.08-0.15 | top-of-funnel               |
| articles            | 1K        | 1.76      | deep engagement (low reach) |
| personal stories    | 8K        | 0.06      | parasocial connection       |

Don't sell with memes. Sell with launches. Use memes to build the audience that sees the launches.
